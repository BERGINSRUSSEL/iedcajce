2-DAY TRAINING WORKSHOP ON
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

IPR Cell of KSCSTE, Amal Jyothi College of Engineering jointly with Patent Information Center - Kerala, Kerala State Council for Science Technology & Environment (KSCSTE) supported by Startups Valley TBI & Makerhub IEDC, Amal Jyothi College of Engineering is organising a 2-DAY TRAINING WORKSHOP ON INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS. Faculty from Engineering Colleges, Scientists from R&D/Scientific Institutions, Officials from Govt.Organizations/Institutions can apply for the Workshop online in the form provided
